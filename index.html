So You Hired a Christian DJ...

What is a 'Christian DJ'?

We don't pray out loud or preach at any shows.

However, we're very familiar with and comfortable playing Christian music, unlike most 'non-Christian DJs.' Some of us simply call ourselves Christian DJs, and some don't. Simply put: Christian DJs are those DJs who are Christians.

What are the Traits of a Christian DJ?

The most noticeable external characteristic of Christian DJs is the way in which we conduct ourselves, and our businesses - according to Christian principles. For example, we will never knowingly play music with vulgar or profane lyrics; or, even songs with immoral subject matter, or that bring dishonor to God in any way. Most importantly: a Christian DJ is one who has a personal relationship with Jesus Christ.

Do You Play Only Christian Music?

No, unless that is what you want. I have the option of not playing any Christian music; it's really up to you!

What Types of Songs Do You Play?

Almost any type! I have thousands of songs spanning all genres of music. However, I use only the edited versions of any contemporary/pop songs that contain profanity to ensure that no offensive words are played. This helps us maintain a positive, fun, family-friendly environment.
