About Josh

A business with big dreams and a bigger God.

About 10 years ago, before I had become a Christian, I worked as a DJ for a small company. However, shortly thereafter I accepted Christ and it didn’t take long to figure out that my faith and the environment that my night job created weren’t going to agree. Someone smarter than me once said, “If you want not to fall, then you ought not walk in slippery places.” I knew something had to change.

In 2007, decided to start a business that honored Jesus Christ, and sought to be the Christian alternative to the mainstream music and perverse agenda that pervades our society. I wanted to build my foundation on the Lord God and chose 'Cornerstone Entertainment' from Ephesians 2:14-22.

By infusing Christian hits from nearly all genres as well as utilizing the “clean” secular chart toppers from today and old, I seek to provide entertainment that the whole family can enjoy. I believe that Christianity is about striving to make Jesus Lord of every aspect of my life including my behavior and my music. I look forward to working together with you to ensure your “perfect” day is just that.

I feel like the Lord has been blessing my business endeavors thus far, and I have only scratched the surface. My business is not just about making money, but about using the talents He has given me to glorify Him. I realize He doesn't need my help with this but I have found He will often use me throughout the St. Louis Bi-State Area, in spite of myself. My sincere prayer is that the best is yet to come, as God‘s Wonders unfold. May Christ Richly Bless You, -Joshua Daniel Boyer

So You Hired a Christian DJ...

What is a 'Christian DJ'?

We don't pray a loud or preach at any shows.

However, we're very familiar with and comfortable playing Christian music, unlike most 'non-Christian DJs.' Some of us simply call ourselves Christian DJs, and some don't. Simply put: Christian DJs are those DJs who are Christians.

What are the Traits of a Christian DJ?

The most noticeable external characteristic of Christian DJs is the way in which we conduct ourselves, and our businesses - according to Christian principles. For example, we will never knowingly play music with vulgar or profane lyrics; or, even songs with immoral subject matter, or that bring dishonor to God in any way. Most importantly: a Christian DJ is one who has a personal relationship with Jesus Christ.

Do You Play Only Christian Music?

No, unless that is what you want. I have the option of not playing any Christian music; it's really up to you!

What Types of Songs Do You Play?

Almost any type; I have thousands of Christian and secular songs. However, I use only the edited versions of any contemporary/pop songs that contain profanity to ensure that no offensive words are played. This helps us maintain a positive, fun, family-friendly environment.
